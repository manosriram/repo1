Advanced Robotics Workshop .

The main idea of Advanced Robotics is to introduce students with the technological world and inspire them about Advancements in Robotics. Today there is a great demand of professionals in the related areas wherein our vision is to provide quality education keeping in minds the methodologies and interest between the students. L-Hub is providing workshops at schools/colleges on PAN India Basis on basic topics related to Robotics.

    Overview :

    Overview This Advanced Robotic workshop will provide school students an advancement to Robotics. In this workshop, the students will learn how to build the task specific robot, and how to drive them, manually as well as automatically. This workshop will guide the students to use the technology in the productive manner. They can build their very own custom instructions for the Robot.

    The duration of this workshop will be two consecutive days with eight hours session each day, in a total of sixteen hours properly divided into theory and hand on practical sessions. At the end of this workshop a competition will be organized among the participating students where each participating student will get Certificate of Participation and the Winners will get Certificate of Merit.
