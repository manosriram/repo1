HTML and CSS Workshop.

HTML is front end basic programming language that lets you design and customize your webpages. CSS is used for designing and styling your webpages. You can learn to use HTML and CSS and make your own website

Workshop Highlights :

- Experienced Faculty.

- Build Website in 2 days.

- Anyone interested can join, as workshop will be conducted from scratch.

- Live projects.

Attractions :

- Certification of Participation to all participants.

- Certificate of Merit to all Winners.

- Enhance your Knowledge through various Live Projects.

    Overview :

    This HTML and CSS workshop will provide students a base to web designing. In this workshop, the students will gain hands-on experience on web designing, effective way of programming, and careers in programming and front end. This workshop will guide the students to use the technology in the productive manner. They can build their very own custom basic websites.

    The duration of this workshop will be two consecutive days with eight hours session each day, in a total of sixteen hours properly divided into theory and hand on practical sessions. At the end of this workshop a competition will be organized among the participating students where each participating student will get Certificate of Participation and the Winners will get Certificate of Merit.
