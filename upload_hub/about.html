Greetings from NK Learnicare Pvt Ltd!!!

A Brief Introduction About the Company:

NK Learnicare is social initiative, whose main focus will be improving education in India and increasing educational exposure within Indian students. Being an NIT Surat Alumnus, we observed the main thing that creates the difference between students of Top and Reputed colleges and other colleges is knowledge exposure available ay Top and Reputed colleges, while other college students lack that thing.
What NK Learnicare will do is just bridge that knowledge exposure, as every student must get benefit to choose their way. NK Learnicare will organize seminars and workshops, and different educational training which are already going in Top and Reputed colleges.
NK Learnicare have tie ups with some companies, and some colleges. If this goes on, then we can assume in coming few years, students will get placements based on their performance and not based on their college value.

Job Location :

Interns are not required to visit the office, so it will be work from home part time internship. However as this is Marketing and sales internship, they may need to travel in their vicinity to engineering colleges, depending on the availability of principals and heads of target schools and colleges and scheduled meetings with them (once or twice in 15 days).

Stipend :

Upto 20,000 INR per month (Strictly Performance Based).

Tentative Start Date : 30th July.

Tenure : 2 Months.

Profile : Marketing and Sales Intern

Selected intern's day-to-day responsibilities include:

  • Contact principals and heads of target schools and colleges and convince them to conduct the workshops and guest lectures at their premises.

  • Once agreed, coordinate with faculties, get coordinators from that college, and publicize about the event to students.

  • Convince the students to participate in the workshops and guest lectures organized by NK Learnicare.

  • Manage the event to make it successful, more the success, more you will be paid.
